# Still Room

A space to slow down. Not a product — a door you open and exhale.

## Philosophy

This website doesn't give people "more."  
It helps them unload.

Every design decision answers one question:  
**Does this make people looser, or tighter?**  
Tight — remove it.

## Three Layers

### Layer 1: The Surface (Landing)
- Full screen. Silence. Dark background.
- One sentence. Slowly fades in. "你来了。"
- No navigation. No buttons. No choices.
- After 3 seconds — a breathing circle appears. Expanding and contracting.
- Purpose: Cut the momentum of rushing.

### Layer 2: Beneath the Surface (Dialogue)
- Click anywhere to enter.
- Not a chatbox. A blank page. Words fade in and out.
- Yang Ding-Yi's skill runs in the background. Responses appear slowly. One sentence at a time.
- No history. No "continue conversation." Every visit starts fresh.
- Purpose: Bring people back from content to presence.

### Layer 3: The Deep (Silence)
- "不说了，坐一会儿。" — Enter pure silence.
- No text. No sound. Just the breathing circle — or still water with ripples.
- Optional timer: 5min / 10min / 15min.
- When time's up: "好了。去吧。" Fades in.
- No summary. No record.
- Purpose: No purpose. That's the point.

## Visual Language

- **Colors**: Deep ink background, warm gray text, occasional warm glow. Like a room at dawn.
- **Typography**: Serif. Not tech. Handwritten feel.
- **Motion**: One thing only — slow. All animations 800ms+. Fade in/out. No bounce. No slide.
- **Sound**: Optional. Ambient. Rain. Water. Wind. Not music.

## Tech

- Single-page application. Lightweight frontend.
- Backend connects to Yang Ding-Yi skill via API.
- Each conversation is a session. Not stored. Not tracked.
- Privacy as compassion — leaving no data is itself the practice.
